Freestyle

Freestyle, also known as front crawl, is the fastest and most efficient of the four competitive swimming strokes. It is characterized by the alternating arm stroke and the flutter kick.

Breaststroke

Breaststroke is a swimming style that is popular among beginners and recreational swimmers. It is characterized by the frog-like leg kick and the simultaneous arm movements.

Backstroke

Backstroke is a swimming style where the swimmer lies on their back and swims with an alternating arm stroke and flutter kick. It is a popular recreational and competitive swimming style.

Butterfly

Butterfly is the most difficult of the four competitive swimming strokes. It is characterized by the simultaneous arm stroke and dolphin kick. It requires a great deal of upper body strength and flexibility.
